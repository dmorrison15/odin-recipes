Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 egg

Directions

  1. Combine Dry Ingredients:

    Sift flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt together in a large bowl.

  2. Add in Wet Ingredients:

    Make a well in the center and add milk, melted butter, and egg; mix until smooth.

  3. Cook Batter:

    Heat a lightly oiled griddle or pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the
    batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake; cook
    until bubbles form and the edges are dry, about 2 to 3 minutes. Flip and cook
    until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.
