Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder, sifted
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar, sifted
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 3/4 teaspoons sea salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup canola oil or olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Directions

  1. Prepare Oven and Baking Dish:

    Preheat the oven to 325Â°F. Lightly spray an 8x8 baking
    dish with cooking spray and line it with parchment paper.
    Spray the parchment paper.

  2. Combine Dry Ingredients:

    In a medium bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa
    powder, powdered sugar, chocolate chips, and salt.

  3. Combine Wet Ingredients:

    In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, olive oil, water,
    and vanilla.

  4. Mix Wet and Dry Ingredients:

    Sprinkle the dry mix over the wet mix and stir until just
    combined.

  5. Bake and Serve:

    Pour the batter into the prepared pan (it'll be thick - that's ok) and use a
    spatula to smooth the top. Bake for 40 to 48 minutes,
    or until a toothpick comes out with only a few crumbs attached.
    Store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days.
